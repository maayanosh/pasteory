Features

⌨️

Global hotkey

⇧⌘V slides a translucent panel up from the bottom of the screen without stealing focus from the app you're using.

🗂️

Card timeline

Text, rich text, images, links, colors, and files — each card color-coded by the app you copied it from.

🔎

Type to search

Just start typing while the panel is open to filter your history in real time.

📌

Pinboards

Organize snippets into named boards that persist outside of history.

⚡

Quick paste

Hold ⌘ to overlay numbers on the first cards; ⌘1–⌘9 pastes instantly. ⇧ pastes as plain text.

🔒

Private by design

No accounts, no sync, no network. Skips password-manager content, with a per-app exclusion list and a pause toggle.

Install

  1. Download Clap.dmg and drag Clap into Applications.
  2. First launch: right-click the app → Open, then confirm. (Clap is open source and ad-hoc signed, not notarized, so macOS shows a one-time warning.)
  3. Grant the Accessibility permission when prompted — that's what lets Clap paste into the app you were using. Without it, Return copies to the clipboard instead.
Where's my data? Clipboard history lives in ~/Library/Application Support/PasteClone/ on your Mac. Nothing ever leaves your machine.

Keyboard shortcuts

⇧⌘VOpen / close the panel
Type anythingFilter history by search
← →Move selection
ReturnPaste the selected item into the previous app
⇧ReturnPaste as plain text
⌘1–9Quick-paste one of the first nine items
SpaceQuick Look preview
⇧⌘NNew pinboard
⌫Delete the selected item
EscClose the panel
